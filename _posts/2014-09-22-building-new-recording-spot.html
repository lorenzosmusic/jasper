We recently started work on a new rehearsal/recording room.

This is the spot where we recorded the video for our song "Picture it all".



About a year ago, we had used this spot to try out an experiment-- to make a mobile electronic headphone based set up. Never really panned out.

This past month, while working on a different way to streamline our recording process, we started to brainstorm and just started building a new room.

Rob and Mark put up a wall

Our plan is for this not only be a place to directly record practices, we will also be working towards a live set up for broadcasting shows.

We hope to have more to share as this moves forward.
