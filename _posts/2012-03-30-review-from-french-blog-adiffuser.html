The French based blog and fellow creative commons enthusiast, @adiffuser, posted a nice review of of our current EP "Just had to let you know".

From twitter:

Lorenzo’s Music – Just Had To Let You Know (un album libre !) -adiffuser.net/musique/lalbum… #ccmusic #rock @lorenzosmusic

a little bluesy and haunting at the edges. The melodies (vocals and guitars) are able to create an oppressive atmosphere, helped by a very dark saxophone. Another very good way.

..We All Fall Down , probably the best song of this EP brought. I like when a stock reaches up gradually in this way (intro on bass, guitar and then came the voice ... and that eventually the battery to run the piece, then taking off with the arrival of other intruments).
