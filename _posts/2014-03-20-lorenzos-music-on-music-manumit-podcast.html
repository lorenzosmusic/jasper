A few weeks ago we appeared on an episode of the +Music Manumit podcast and I just realized I completely forgot to post it here.

The podcast is one that I've actually been co-hosting for a few years so it was a bit weird being on it as one of the people in the bands we talk to. 

But that weirdness aside, we had a good time and I think it's an entertaining listen. We talk about the recent singles "Low" and "Picture it all" as well as our current single release process.

And as most conversations we have, go off on many, many different tangents.

Enjoy!
