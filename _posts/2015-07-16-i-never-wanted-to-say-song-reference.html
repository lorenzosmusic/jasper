When working on a song I like to keep a playlist of songs for reference.

These aren't always about the songs. It could be anything from them I want to remember. Sounds, structure, subject, mood, etc..

I bring them up and add to the list as we go along.

Its interesting to listen after song is finished. The strange mix tape it has become.

Wanted to share one that I made for previous single release of the song "I never wanted to say".
Which you can download here. 

Video Mix Tape Songs:
  1. "Good Time Roll" by The Cars
  2. "I Can't Go For That (No Can Do)" by Hall & Oats
  3. "Would That Not Be Nice" by Divine Fits
  4. "Atomic Bomb" by William Onyeabor
  5. "Popcorn" by Hot Butter
  6. "Give Life Back to Music" by Daft Punk 
  7. "I Never Wanted to Say" by Lorenzo's Music
