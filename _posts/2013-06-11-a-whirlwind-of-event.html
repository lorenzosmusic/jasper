The week before our June 1st show in honor of our friend Paulie Heenan was and emotional and rewarding experience. This Photo above is from the tribute set we did at the end of the night with some of his friends/musicians performing unheard songs he had written before his passing.

We love him and miss him and feel this really honored the way he should be remembered.

There was live video and audio recorded of all that bands that had performed and I know that they all hope to release these soon.

We got our hands on a recording of our song "Fervor" that we have always felt was much better as a live song compared to the studio recording.
