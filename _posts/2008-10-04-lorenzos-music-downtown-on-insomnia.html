“Downtown” is a fantastic piece of work that makes me feel like I’m listening to a long lost track from The English Beat. Instead of the standard ska horns, you are going to hear some oft-neglected saxophone punctuated with some upbeat ska guitar & drum “pick-it-ups”, all overlayed with the distinct vocals of Tom Ray and some brilliant harmonization and frenzied instrumental execution throughout.
