This Sunday at 2pm Lorenzo's Music will be on "Support Your Scene". A local music show on Madison Student Radio WSUM 91.7 FM promoting songs from our new album "Bazar", which you can download for free on our audio page.
WSUM is very pro-local music. They have shows dedicated to local music, and love to work with local (and not-so-local) bands.
We did an impromptu performance on the show last year showcasing some of the songs from the new album that we were in the studio recording at the time. You can listen to that whole show here on our website.

If you're not in the Madison area on Sunday Sept. 14th at 2pm you can still listen to the show online at WSUM.org
