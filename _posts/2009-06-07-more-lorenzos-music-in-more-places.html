You've always been able to listen and download our songs at last.fm. But now we've added our albums to a bunch of other great online music services that also allow us to stay true to our creative commons license that our albums are released through.

Jamendo.com
Jamendo is a community of free, legal and unlimited music published under Creative Commons licenses.

iLike.com
enables fans to discover and share playlists, new music that matches their personal tastes, and concerts by their favorite artists.

Grooveshark.com
Grooveshark is a revolutionary music community that rewards users for sharing their music. A virtual online music jukebox.

Bandcamp.com
offers tracks in mp3, AAC, FLAC, Ogg...all on demand.

Enjoy.
