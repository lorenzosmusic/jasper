I think music should be shared and appreciated and I've always felt hypocritical when we were only letting people download one or two songs. When I get a chance to listen to something and truly enjoy it, I tell everyone I know about it. Usually what happens, days pass and I run into someone who then tells me about it, who heard it from this person who heard it from this one etc... I think why should this be any different. So let's give it a try.

Now, this music is under Creative Commons License. Which means that you can download it, share it, burn it and use it in a non-commercial manner. Basically, if you want to use it in a way that makes money you have to ask our permission first. But if you want to use it in a blog or personal podcast or background music for a personal video that's okay, you just have to give us credit for it.

Help promote this post at digg.com

Hear all about it in the podcast below.

All we ask is that you share this and give and trade with anyone you know.

You can email us and let us know what you think or click the button below and send us a voice email and tell us, directly from your computer.
