So this video showed up on our feed and I was confused at first. Now don't get me wrong, I was amazed at all the crazy stuff that these guys were doing on Pogo-Sticks but was still wondering why this video showed up. Then about 30 seconds into it, the music changed and they were playing our song "You Got To Feel It Tonight"!

Now that's pretty cool. Suddenly I feel like my dream as a kid, to be one of the bands on the old Santa Cruz skateboard video "Wheels Of Fire" has been fulfilled in a way.
