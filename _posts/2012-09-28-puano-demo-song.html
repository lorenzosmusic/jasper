We've got a single we wrote called Puano that we're really excited about and wanted to share it with you.

This song started one night while Mark and I were setting up in the back studio. There's a piano in the room and Mark sat down and started playing what became the lead line of the song. While he was playing I got behind some drums and came up for the rhythm for it.

Luckily, we had the recorder running and were able to get the whole thing down. As far as the song name- it's called Puano because when we titled the file we recorded, we were just going to call it "Piano" to remember it. But I mistyped the word. When we noticed this later we thought it was kinda funny and kept it.

This is an exclusive because it's a demo version of the song. It may change over time and you can have a copy that may not even be the version we release. It could change dramatically!

In that same train of thought let us know what you think, and leave a comment below.
